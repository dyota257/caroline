I am participating in Dry Spell for MATES

SPONSOR ME

Starting May 8th, I will be going 50 days sugar free, to raise awareness of and funds for MATES in Construction WA. MATES work to reduce the disarmingly high level of suicide among Australian construction workers.


Australia loses a construction worker every second day to suicide.

 

By sponsoring me in this effort you are MATES in Construction WA to provide suicide prevention through:

> community development programs on site

> supporting workers in need through case management

> a 24/7 help line 


It would mean a lot to me if you would donate to my Fundraising Page.

 

Cheers (but in a non-alcoholic beverage kind of way)!

 

* Some participants may choose to do a 'dry spell' from something else (100 days free of sugar, coffee, gambling etc).

 

If you or anyone you know are finding things tough, call the MATES Helpline 24/7 (1300 642 111) or in emergency call 000.

For more information about MATES in Construction, visit their website: https://mates.org.au/.
